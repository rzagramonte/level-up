Classes at Level Up

Come experience the difference being in one of our classes makes. We’ll help you get signed up for any class you’d like to take! At Level Up, we are nurturers: we seek only to encourage, entertain, and empower. Here we keep open minds. There is no one type or way in our diverse community. Come as you are! We know serious fitness is hard, but that doesn’t mean it can’t be an edge-of-your-seat, can’t-get-enough, look-forward-to-your-workouts party.

Members have the ability to book classes in advance. All classes are free for members and single visit passes include one class. All fitness levels are welcome! Gym classes are a great way of keeping those motivation levels high and ensuring that you get the most out of every workout. Mixing up your gym routine is also a great way of keeping things interesting.
